A B O U T   M E

I am compelled to learn, to be challenged, and to create. With a professional background of over 10 years in private security and a lifetime of creative passion projects, a desire for further growth and development led me to studying Python in my spare time.


The breakthrough moment where the seemingly impossible is achieved has instilled in me a love for programming, and inspired me to enrol in General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive course. The blending of methodical, logical planning and execution with creative, outside-the-box expression has made software development a beautiful medium to me, and one I relish the continued opportunity to work with.

P E R S O N A L   I N T E R E S T S

I have over 15 years of experience with amateur graphic design and image manipulation, and have previously done such work as designing logos and avatars for forums and social media profiles, custom texture files for video game modifications, and mockups and audio-visual resources for my personal projects.


I also harbour a deep and abiding fascination with retro/vintage AV systems, and have invested significant time in learning how to restore them to good working condition – most recently, a 1979 Sony Walkman cassette player, a 1982 Thorens turntable, and a 1995 Sony Playstation game console.


I am an avid creative writer, having twice participated in and completed the annual National Novel Writing Month event, as well as previously contributing film reviews to amateur publications and continuing to develop three ongoing personal projects.
